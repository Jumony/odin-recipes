Raspberry Juice

Description

Raspberry Juice with concentrate. A refreshing drink for many occasions

Ingredients
  • Glass
  • 2 Cups of Raspberries
  • 1/4 Cup of Sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon of Lemon Juice
  • 2 Cups of Water
  • a Few Extra Raspberries for Concentrate
Steps
  1. Wash the raspberries
  2. Make a syrup: In a small saucepan, combine 1/4 cup of sugar with 1/2 cup of water.
    Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring until the suagr completely dissolves. this creates a simple
    syrup. Let it cool
  3. Blend the Raspberries: Place the washed raspberries in a blender. Add the lemon juice and the cooked simple
    syrup. Blend until you have a smooth mixture
  4. Pour into cup and dilute with water: Pour the juice into a pitcher. Add 1.5 cups of cold water and stir
    well. Adjust the water quantity based on your taste preference. You can add more
    water if you prefer a lighter juice.
  5. Chill and Serve
