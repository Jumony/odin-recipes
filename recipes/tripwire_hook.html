FROM MINECRAFT - The Tripwire Hook

Description

A tripwire hook is connected by another tripwire hook on
the opposite side connected by strings and emit a redstone
signal when an entity walks through it

Ingredients
  • 1 Iron Ingot
  • 1 Stick
  • 1 Wooden Plank
Steps
  1. Obtain an Iron ingot which is obtained through going underground
    in caves, etc. and then searching for a stone block with light pink specs
    *These ores can only be obtained with a stone pickaxe or higher*
    After obtaining these ores, place them into the furnace and smelt it
  2. Obtain a wooden stick: These are obtained by cutting down trees
    Putting one log into the crafting table to make 4 wooden planks and then align
    two wooden planks in a vertical line to get 4 sticks
  3. Obtain a wooden plank: Obtained through breaking down a tree, getting
    its log and putting it into the crafting area to get 4 planks
  4. In a crafting table, align the three items completely vertically, with
    the plank on the bottom, stick in the middle, and iron ingot on the top
  5. You now have a tripwire hook!
